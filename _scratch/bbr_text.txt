5 Process Notes 
5.1  The day of the inoculation is N-2 Seed Day 0. The day of medium dilution is N-1 Seed Day 0. 
5.2  For the purpose of this record, 1 kg = 1 L, unless stated otherwise. 
5.3  Record all decimals to the place indicated by the number of Xs, i.e., for “X.XX”, record two decimal places and for 
“X.X”, record to one decimal place. 
5.4  Contact the area supervisor/designee if any of the operating conditions are outside of the specified range.  
5.5  When proceeding with comments, the supervisor/designee is responsible for contacting the appropriate 
departments (i.e. Quality, CCFS-TT) to make an assessment. The process decisions need to be documented in 
Attachment 10: Comments Section.  
5.6  Airflow rates and agitation speeds defined within this MPR are initial set points. These set points can be adjusted 
at the supervisor/designee's discretion. Record all chages in Attachment 10: Comments Section. 
5.7  Airflow rates for both sparge and overlay and agitation speeds are defined as initial set points.  These set points 
can be adjusted at the supervisor/designee's discretion, as long as the pO2 level is maintained within range.  
Record all changes on Attachment 10: Comments Section. 
5.8  The initial pO2 alarm set points can be adjusted at the supervisor/designee's discretion, as long as the new 
alarm set points are within pO2 specified process limits. A supervisor/designee’s signature is required and all 
changes need to be documented in Attachment 10: Comments Section. 
5.9  Whenever the BGA is unavailable, measure pH levels using the Benchtop pH and Conductivity meter per SOP -
0080297. Record the temperature compensated values denoted with (T), if applicable.  
5.10  Minimize medium and nutrient feed exposure to light during storage or use. 
5.11  Whenever sample bags/flasks are used for sampling, record the information in Attachment 1:  Sample 
Assembly Attachment and Sampling. 
5.12  Whenever a benchtop balance or floor scale is used, perform a weight check before use per SOP-0107155 or 
SOP-0080294. 
5.13  Whenever a sealer is used, operate equipment per SOP-0080812 or SOP-0080522.  
5.14  Additional samples for pH monitoring may be taken at supervisor/designee’s discretion. Record the results in  
Attachment 6:  Additional pH Monitoring - pH samples’ information. 
5.15  All Sections leading up to Media Charge and TFF Priming operation can be performed concurrently.
